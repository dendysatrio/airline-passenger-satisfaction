# ✈️ Airline Passenger Satisfaction Analysis

End-to-end data analysis on 129,880 airline passenger records to identify 
key drivers of dissatisfaction and support data-driven service improvement.

**Program:** RevoU Full Stack Data Analytics — DEEP Project  
**Period:** Oct 2025 – Feb 2026

---

## 🎯 Business Context

The airline invests across multiple service and operational improvement 
initiatives but lacks clear, data-driven prioritization of which factors 
most strongly influence passenger satisfaction. This analysis was conducted 
for the **Head of Customer Experience** to support better resource allocation.

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

- **Operational reliability and service execution** emerged as the dimensions 
  most strongly associated with passenger dissatisfaction
- **Digital experience** (e.g. in-flight WiFi) contributes to dissatisfaction 
  but plays a secondary role compared to service execution
- **Physical comfort** ratings showed clear separation between satisfied and 
  dissatisfied passenger groups
- A baseline driver gap analysis was used to prioritize which service areas 
  have the largest room for improvement
